WHO ARE WE?

Orangutan is an Esports organization formed from a collective of passionate dreamers who have been working towards the creation of not only an Esports Organization, but an Esports Brand. Headquartered in Mumbai, we comprise an enthusiastic workforce that functions behind the scenes to curate a seamless experience for all our athletes. Our everyday motto is to make people realize the unlimited potential that Esports has, and that is why our fans are at the core of everything we do.
